# Privacy Policy for Song Streak

**Effective Date:** December 19, 2025
**Last Updated:** December 19, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **Song Streak**. We are committed to protecting your personal data and your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("Song Streak" or the "App").

This policy complies with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), the EU General Data Protection Regulation (EU GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other applicable data protection laws.

## 2. Data Controller

For the purposes of data protection laws, the developer of Song Streak is the "Data Controller" responsible for your personal data.

## 3. Data We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide you with a functional music practice companion. Here is exactly what we collect:

### A. Account Data

- **Email address:** Required for account creation (via email/password, Google Sign-In, or Apple Sign-In).
- **Name:** Optional, only if you choose to provide it during social sign-in.
- **Profile picture:** Optional, only if you choose to upload one.

### B. Song Library Data

- Song title, artist, and duration
- Video URLs (for tutorial reference)
- Instrument type and genres
- Music theory data: tuning, key, tempo (BPM), time signature, chords, scales, techniques
- Album artwork URL (fetched from iTunes)
- Lyrics (fetched on-demand from third-party services)

### C. Practice Data

- Practice session duration and timestamps
- Total practice time per song
- Achievement unlock dates and milestones

### D. Band Collaboration Data

- Band name and membership
- Your role in each band (creator or member)
- Setlist information (song assignments, venue, gig dates)

### E. Device Data

- Basic device information (model, OS version) for compatibility purposes only
- App settings preferences (sound enabled, theme preference) stored locally on your device

**What We Do NOT Collect:**

- We do NOT use analytics or tracking services
- We do NOT collect location data
- We do NOT use advertising networks
- We do NOT sell your data to anyone

## 4. Device Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions, all of which are optional:

- **Camera:** Only used if you choose to take a profile picture. We do not access your camera otherwise.
- **Photo Library:** Only used if you choose to select a profile picture from your photos.
- **Microphone:** Reserved for potential future audio practice features. Currently not actively used.

## 5. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the App's functionality:

- **Account management:** To create and maintain your account.
- **Core features:** To save your songs, track practice time, and calculate achievements.
- **Band collaboration:** To enable shared setlists with your bandmates.
- **AI analysis:** To extract music theory from video tutorials you provide.

## 6. Legal Basis for Processing

Under GDPR, we process your data based on the following legal grounds:

- **Contractual Necessity:** To provide the App's core services (saving songs, tracking practice).
- **Consent:** For optional features (profile picture, band collaboration).
- **Legitimate Interests:** To maintain security and improve the App.

## 8. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers with encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS). While we strive to keep data within the UK/EEA, some third-party services may process data in the United States. Where data is transferred outside the UK/EEA, appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) are in place.

Some data (app settings, authentication session) is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers.

## 9. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data only for as long as you have an active account. When you delete your account:

- All your personal data is permanently deleted within **30 days**.
- This includes: your song library, practice history, achievements, and band memberships.
- This action is irreversible.

You can delete your account at any time from the App's settings menu.

## 10. Your Privacy Rights

### For UK/EU Users (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

- **Right to Access:** Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
- **Right to Rectification:** Request correction of inaccurate data.
- **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion of your personal data.
- **Right to Portability:** Request your data in a machine-readable format.
- **Right to Object:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
- **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent at any time for consent-based processing.
- **Right to Complain:** Lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.

### For California Users (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, you have the following rights:

- **Right to Know:** Request what personal information we collect, use, and disclose.
- **Right to Delete:** Request deletion of your personal information.
- **Right to Opt-Out:** Opt-out of the sale of personal information. *Note: We do NOT sell your personal information.*
- **Right to Non-Discrimination:** We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

### For All Users

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at ihitge@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

## 11. Children's Privacy

Song Streak is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. Age verification is handled through the authentication providers (Apple, Google) which require users to meet their minimum age requirements.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

## 12. Cookies & Local Storage

The Song Streak mobile app does not use cookies. We use local device storage (AsyncStorage) to save your preferences and authentication session. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly sync with our servers.

The Song Streak website uses minimal cookies for essential functionality (such as remembering your cookie consent preference). We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.
