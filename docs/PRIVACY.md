# Privacy Policy for Song Streak

**Effective Date:** December 18, 2025  
**Last Updated:** December 18, 2025

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to **Song Streak**. We are committed to protecting your personal data and your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("Song Streak" or the "App"), in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

## 2. Who is the Data Controller?
For the purposes of the UK GDPR, the developer of Song Streak is the "Data Controller".

## 3. Data We Collect
We collect information that allows you to manage your music repertoire and track practice progress.

### A. Information You Provide to Us:
*   **Account Information**: When you sign up, we collect your email address and password (managed via Supabase Auth).
*   **Profile Data**: If you choose to upload an avatar or provide a username.
*   **User Content**: Information about the songs you add, including titles, artists, and YouTube URLs.
*   **Practice Data**: Data about your practice sessions (duration, dates, streaks, and achievements).

### B. Automatically Collected Data:
*   **Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with the App (e.g., which buttons you press, time spent on screens).
*   **Device Info**: Basic device information (model, OS version) to ensure compatibility and provide haptic/audio feedback.

## 4. How We Process Your Data (Legal Basis)
Under UK GDPR, we process your data based on the following:
*   **Contractual Necessity**: To provide you with the App's core services (saving songs, tracking streaks).
*   **Consent**: For optional features like uploading a profile picture.
*   **Legitimate Interests**: To improve the App’s performance and ensure security.

## 5. Third-Party Service Processors
We use reputable third-party services to help run the App. These services are "Data Processors" and have their own privacy policies:
*   **Supabase (Database & Auth)**: Stores your account and practice data.
*   **Google Gemini (AI Analysis)**: We process YouTube URLs through Gemini AI to extract song metadata. Note: We do not send your personal account data to Gemini, only the video URL for analysis.
*   **iTunes API**: Used to fetch album artwork.

## 6. Where Your Data is Stored
Your data is primarily stored on servers provided by Supabase. While we strive to keep data within the UK/EEA, some third-party services may process data in the US. We ensure appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) are in place.

## 7. Data Retention
We retain your data for as long as you have an active account. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data from our active databases within 30 days, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

## 8. Your Rights (UK GDPR)
You have the following rights:
*   **Right to Access**: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
*   **Right to Rectification**: Request that we correct any inaccurate data.
*   **Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")**: Request that we delete your personal data.
*   **Right to Portability**: Request a copy of your data in a structured format.
*   **Right to Withdraw Consent**: Where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at ihitge@gmail.com.

## 9. Children’s Privacy
Song Streak is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

## 10. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the App or via email.
